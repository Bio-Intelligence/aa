Early Life

Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany, to a middle-class Jewish family. He showed an early interest in science and mathematics, particularly fascinated by a geometry book at the age of twelve. Despite his brilliance, he struggled in school due to a rigid educational system that did not cater to his curiosity.
