Later Life

Einstein moved to the United States in 1933, fleeing the rise of the Nazi regime in Germany. He accepted a position at the Institute for Advanced Study in Princeton, New Jersey, where he continued his research until his death on April 18, 1955. Throughout his life, Einstein was also known for his advocacy on social and political issues, including pacifism and civil rights
